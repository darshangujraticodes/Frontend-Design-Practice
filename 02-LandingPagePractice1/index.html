The Legacy of
Manjri Stud Farm

The home of freedom and flight

At the heart of Kingstown lies the rich and regal legacy of the Manjri Stud Farm. Evoking a charmed life, it is one of the oldest equestrian centres in the country. The stud farm was originally established by Sir Sultan Chinoy. It was then passed into the hands of the Scindia royal family. Shapoorji Pallonji Group then bought the stud farm from the Scindia family. The stud farm’s beautiful landscape, dotted with century-old trees, retains its original heritage architecture, maintained in all its historic glory.
